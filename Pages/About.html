Hello and welcome to my site! Pull up a seat and make yourself at home.

I'm Bianca, a creative media professional specializing in advertising, project management, and video production.

I have an appreciation for all things creative - whether it's seeing a great Broadway show, snapping pictures of whatever catches my eye, making a scrapbook of my latest travels, and even juggling (more than just my hobbies)!

In 2011, I received a Bachelor's degree in Film and Media Arts from Temple University and have since gained years of experience working for digital media technology startups in the Philadelphia area. Working in creative startup environments has given me the opportunity to "wear many hats" (blue, spotted, striped, tall, and small) and build a diverse skill set.

Currently, I work with a global online community of over 80,000 creatives at Poptent - a crowd-sourced video production company. Here, I manage multiple video projects, correspond daily with brand clients and filmmakers, and lead marketing initiatives to engage new talent on the web and in person.

In 2010, I was a Production Assistant for WHYY's Emmy award-winning TV show, Friday Arts. This involved working closely with the producers of the show, going on-location for production, and assisting with edits in the cutting room.

I love meeting new people, and I think it would be awesome to hear from you. Plus, I'm a huge short-film buff, so if you know of any good ones send them my way.
